Fabrics

Choosing the correct fabric for a project is essential. Each different material gives the garment a different aestetic. A list of pros and cons for the most common fabrics can be can be found below.

Material Pro Con
Cotton It is breathable and versitile. It tends to bleed and shrink
Polyester It is durable while also being flexible. Enviormentally damaging to produce.
Satin It natural and hypoallergenic. It is slippery and difficult to sew.
Spandex It is lightweight and comfortable. It is sensitive to heat and shrinkage.
