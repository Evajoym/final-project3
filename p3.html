Sewing Essentials

Sewing can be an expensive hobby. Many different items and materials are need to effectivly work. Below is a list of the items needed to intially begin sewing.

  • Sewing sheers
  • Sewing Machine
  • Measuring tape
  • Rotary cutter
  • Chalk
  • Tread
  • Fabric
  • Seam ripper
  • Iron
